The Honest Woodcutter was a poor man who lived near a forest. One day, he accidentally dropped his axe into a river and felt very sad. A fairy appeared and helped him by showing a golden and silver axe, but he refused both because they were not his. He only accepted his own iron axe. The fairy was happy with his honesty and rewarded him with all three axes. The story teaches us that honesty is always rewarded.
